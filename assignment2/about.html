Hello, I'm Irene

I'm originally from Montreal, Canada. I graduated from Concordia University with a degree in Psychology and minor in Linguistics in 2017. While a student there, I worked as a research assistant in the Psycholinguistics & Cognition Lab led by Dr. Roberto G. de Almeida, and this was where my interest in Human Factors was born.

After graduating, I found myself taking on a teaching role on the JET Programme, working as an English teacher for 3 years in Akita, Japan. There, I also became a leader of my expatriate community.

Currently, I am a student at George Brown College in Interactive Media Management, and I look forward to a future in project management in design.
